@makershour Q5 If a friend/relative wanted to get into some sort of crafting or making, what present might you get for them to help get them started, and why?

If it was knitting, then I would get them a set of regular (not circular or DPN) knitting needles, plus yarn that is very easy to work with (not fuzzy or anything confusing), with the sizes matched. Ideally I would give them BIG yarn and needles, so they could feel like they were making progress, and not squint!
